中國對Meta收購Manus展開調查

China Is Investigating Meta’s Latest A.I. Acquisition

MEAGHAN TOBIN, XINYUN WU, ELI TAN • 2026-01-09
China is investigating whether Meta’s acquisition of the artificial intelligence startup Manus violated its laws on technology exports and outbound investment.
中國正對Meta收購人工智慧初創公司Manus是否違反其技術出口和對外投資相關法律展開調查。
Those rules say that the Chinese government must approve the export of certain technologies, including interactive artificial intelligence systems. Manus is based in Singapore but was founded by Chinese engineers and had a Chinese parent company. Beijing has used the same grounds to claim final sign-off on any sale of TikTok’s U.S. operations.
這些法規要求，某些技術的出口必須獲得中國政府批准，其中包括交互式人工智慧系統。Manus總部設在新加坡，但由中國工程師創立，且其母公司在中國。北京此前也曾以同樣的理由主張對TikTok美國業務的任何出售擁有最終審批權。
China’s Ministry of Commerce is evaluating whether Meta violated those rules when it acquired Manus last month, He Yadong, a ministry spokesman, said on Thursday.
中國商務部發言人何亞東週四表示，商務部正在評估Meta上個月收購Manus是否違反了上述規定。
Manus drew attention from Silicon Valley last March when it launched an A.I. agent, or tool, that could be directed to build websites and do other basic coding tasks on its own. At the time, the American tech industry was reeling from the announcement that the Chinese startup DeepSeek had created a high-performing A.I. system for far less than leading U.S. companies had spent. By December, Manus said it had surpassed $100 million in annual recurring revenue.
去年3月，Manus推出了一款人工智慧代理（即工具），可以自主構建網站並完成其他基礎編碼任務，這引起了矽谷的關注。當時，美國科技行業正因中國初創公司DeepSeek宣布以遠低於美國領先企業的成本打造出高性能人工智慧系統而震動。截至去年12月，Manus表示其年度經常性收入已超過1億美元。
廣告
Meta’s deal for Manus capped a year of extravagant spending by the Silicon Valley company on artificial intelligence researchers.
Meta收購Manus的交易為這家矽谷公司在人工智慧研究人員方面大手筆投入的一年畫上了句號。
Last June, Meta made its largest investment since its acquisition of WhatsApp in 2014 when it invested $14.3 billion in Scale AI, a start-up that works with data to train artificial intelligence systems. Meta did not disclose the terms of its acquisition of Manus.
去年6月，Meta進行了自2014年收購WhatsApp以來最大的一筆投資，向從事人工智慧系統訓練數據業務的初創公司Scale AI投資了143億美元。Meta未披露收購Manus的具體條款。
China’s investigation into the transaction comes as questions swirl in Washington and Beijing about the fate of TikTok’s U.S. operations and whether regulators will block Chinese companies from gaining access to A.I. chips made by Nvidia, the American chip maker.
中國對這筆交易展開調查之際，華盛頓和北京正在關注TikTok美國業務的命運，以及監管機構是否會阻止中國企業獲得美國晶片製造商英偉達生產的人工智慧晶片。
Officials in Beijing have pushed companies in China to buy domestic chips and warned that Nvidia’s chips may carry risks.
北京官員敦促中國企業購買國產晶片，並警告稱英偉達的晶片可能存在風險。
For many Chinese tech start-ups, the aspiration is to launch the next global hit product, like TikTok. Yet none want to end up like the Chinese parent of TikTok, ByteDance, which saw its executives questioned before Congress about the company’s ties to the Chinese government.
對許多中國科技初創企業而言，打造下一個像TikTok那樣的全球爆款產品是共同願景。但沒有公司願意重蹈TikTok中國母公司字節跳動的覆轍——其高管曾因公司與中國政府的關係而在美國國會接受質詢。
The prospect of intense scrutiny from regulators in both Washington and Beijing pushes Chinese entrepreneurs to choose between two paths for growth: catering to the Chinese market or pursuing a global audience outside China. For those without access to international investors, the first is the only feasible option.
來自華盛頓和北京監管機構的雙重嚴密審查迫使中國創業者在兩條增長路徑之間作出選擇：要麼深耕中國市場，要麼面向中國以外的全球用戶。對於無法接觸國際投資者的企業來說，前者往往是唯一可行的選擇。
廣告
Chinese officials need to weigh these enterprises’ prospects for growth when drawing up the country’s technology export regulations, Cui Fan, a professor at the University of International Business and Economics in Beijing, wrote in a blog post.
北京對外經濟貿易大學教授崔凡在一篇部落格文章中寫道，中國官員在制定技術出口監管規則時需要權衡這些企業的增長前景。
Meta and Manus did not immediately respond to requests for comment.
Meta和Manus均未立即回應置評請求。
Manus trained its agent using A.I. systems built by other companies including Alibaba of China and Anthropic of the United States. Two of the company’s early investors were former employees of Robinhood, the investing app.
在訓練其人工智慧代理時，Manus使用了包括中國阿里巴巴和美國Anthropic在內的多家公司開發的人工智慧系統。該公司的早期投資者包括投資應用Robinhood的兩名前員工。
