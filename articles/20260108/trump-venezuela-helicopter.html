突襲委內瑞拉，美軍直升機曾經歷驚險一刻

A Close Call for U.S. Commandos and an Emboldened Trump

ERIC SCHMITT, GREG JAFFE • 2026-01-08
WASHINGTON — President Donald Trump described the operation to capture President Nicolás Maduro of Venezuela as a “perfectly executed” display of American military power.
唐納德·川普總統稱，抓捕委內瑞拉總統馬杜羅的行動是美國軍事力量「完美執行」的展現。
But Trump’s account of the audacious raid left out key details that spotlit the risk U.S. troops faced as they approached Maduro’s fortified compound and how close the high-stakes operation came to taking a turn for the worse.
但川普對這場大膽突襲的描述省略了一些關鍵細節，從中凸顯美軍在逼近馬杜羅戒備森嚴的官邸時面臨的風險，以及這場高風險行動險些惡化的驚險過程。
In the early hours of Saturday morning, U.S. Army helicopters skimmed 100 feet above the sea and then over Caracas, Venezuela’s capital, racing toward their target. Their stealthy pathway had been cleared by an American cyberattack that darkened the city, and by radar-evading U.S. fighter jets that pounded Venezuela’s Russian-built air defenses.
上週六凌晨，美軍直升機在離海面約30米的低空掠過，飛越委內瑞拉首都卡拉卡斯，直奔目標而去。美軍先通過網路攻擊導致該市斷電陷入黑暗，再出動隱形戰鬥機猛烈打擊委內瑞拉的俄制防空系統，為直升機開闢了隱蔽通道。
Initially, the helicopters, carrying dozens of Army Delta Force commandos, flew undetected.
起初，搭載數十名陸軍三角洲特種部隊突擊隊員的直升機未被發現。
廣告
But as they approached Maduro’s lair, the aircraft came under fire and shot back. The first helicopter in the assault, a giant twin-rotor MH-47 Chinook, was hit but remained flyable. The flight leader, who also planned the mission and was piloting the Chinook, was struck three times in the leg, said current and former U.S. officials, who spoke on condition of anonymity to discuss sensitive matters.
但在逼近馬杜羅的住所時，直升機遭到火力攻擊，美軍隨即還擊。參與突襲的首架直升機是一架大型雙旋翼MH-47支奴乾直升機，機身被擊中但保持了飛行能力。據因討論敏感事宜而要求匿名的現任和前任美國官員透露，擔任飛行領隊的任務策劃者兼支奴乾駕駛員腿部三處中彈。
As the damaged helicopter struggled to stay aloft and deliver its troops to their target, the success of the entire operation, called Absolute Resolve, involving more than 150 aircraft launched from 20 different land sea bases in the region, hung in the balance.
這架受損直升機艱難維持飛行，努力將突擊隊員送達目標地點，而這場涉及從該地區20個不同陸海基地起飛的150多架飛機、名為「絕對決心」的行動，成敗就維繫於此。
In his second term, Trump has grown more confident in sending the military on high-stakes missions to achieve complex foreign policy objectives. Asked what he would do if Delcy Rodríguez, Venezuela’s interim leader, resists his administration’s directives, Trump threatened another raid.
在第二任期內，川普愈發自信地派遣軍隊執行高風險任務，以實現複雜的外交目標。當被問及若委內瑞拉臨時領導人羅德里格斯拒絕其政府指令會採取何種措施時，川普威脅發動另一場襲擊。
“She will face a situation probably worse than Maduro,” Trump told reporters on Air Force One this week.
「她面臨的局面可能比馬杜羅更糟，」川普本週在「空軍一號」上對記者表示。
But Trump’s string of military successes in places like Iran, Syria and now Venezuela, coupled with his tendency to take big risks, dating back to the earliest days of his real estate career and multiple bankruptcies, have obscured the downsides of using force in the manner that has increasingly defined his foreign policy.
但川普在伊朗、敘利亞以及如今委內瑞拉等地接連取得的軍事勝利，加之他自房地產職業生涯初期乃至多次破產時展現出的冒險傾向，掩蓋了其對外政策中日益依賴武力的弊端。
“You’re operating on a very delicate wire and the risks of failing are huge,” said Seth G. Jones, a senior national security expert at the Center for Strategic and International Studies. “Sometimes there are factors beyond your control.”
「你就像在走一根非常脆弱的鋼絲，失敗的風險極大，」戰略與國際研究中心高級國家安全專家塞思·瓊斯表示。「有時會出現超出你控制的因素。」
Those risks of a potentially catastrophic outcome were especially evident in Venezuela as the pilots of the damaged Chinook — one of them seriously wounded — fought to complete their mission.
在委內瑞拉行動中，這種可能導致災難性後果的風險尤為明顯——受損的支奴乾直升機的飛行員是奮力才完成任務，其中一人身受重傷。
Would these operators from the Army’s elite 160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment adjust and prevail, as members of the Seal Team 6 raid to capture Osama bin Laden in Pakistan in 2011 did after one of their helicopters clipped a wall and crashed?
這些來自陸軍精銳第160特種作戰航空團的機組人員是否能夠像2011年海豹六隊突襲巴基斯坦抓捕賓·拉登時所做的那樣，迅速調整並成功完成任務？（當時一架直升機撞上牆壁墜毀。）
廣告
Or would the Chinook plummet into a hostile city and become a deadly echo of the Black Hawk helicopter that was shot down in Mogadishu, Somalia, in 1993 and ignited a fierce battle in which 18 U.S. troops died and 73 were wounded, at the time the deadliest single engagement for American troops since the Vietnam War?
這架支奴乾有沒有可能墜入充滿敵意的城市，重蹈1993年索馬利亞摩加迪沙黑鷹直升機被擊落的覆轍？當時的事件引發激烈戰鬥，18名美軍士兵陣亡、73人受傷，成為越戰以來美軍單次交戰中傷亡最慘重的一役。
“Failure of one component of this well-oiled machine would have endangered the entire mission,” Gen. Dan Caine, chair of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, said only hours after the mission was complete.
「這台精密機器的任何一個部件出現故障，都可能危及整個任務，」參謀長聯席會議主席丹·凱恩將軍在任務完成僅數小時後表示。
The Chinook did not crash. The flight leader, with the help of a copilot, stuck the landing, disgorged the commandos inside and guided the aircraft back to the warship Iwo Jima off the Venezuelan coast, as search-and-rescue teams and quick-reaction forces outside the country stood ready to zoom in, if needed.
這架支奴乾最終沒有墜毀。飛行指揮官在副駕駛的協助下成功著陸，讓機上突擊隊員迅速下機，隨後駕駛受損直升機返回委內瑞拉海岸附近的硫磺島號軍艦。與此同時，境外待命的搜救隊和快速反應部隊已做好隨時介入的準備。
By 2:01 a.m. in Caracas, more than 80 Army commandos had piled out of the helicopters, including the damaged Chinook. After an intense firefight with Maduro’s Cuban security detail, the soldiers blew open a door leading to Maduro’s bedroom, where they seized him and his wife as they were trying to escape into a steel-reinforced room.
卡拉卡斯時間凌晨2點01分，包括受損直升機上的隊員在內，80多名陸軍突擊隊員全部跳下直升機。在與馬杜羅的古巴保鏢隊激烈交火後，士兵們炸開了通往馬杜羅卧室的大門，在他和妻子試圖逃入一間鋼鐵加固房間時將二人抓獲。
A fresh wave of helicopters whisked the commandos and their prisoners from the compound, fighting through hostile fire on the way out. By 4:29 a.m., the aircraft were back over water, and later delivered Maduro and his wife to authorities on the Iwo Jima.
新一批直升機隨即趕來，護送突擊隊員及其俘虜離開官邸，撤離途中仍遭遇敵對火力攻擊。凌晨4點29分，直升機重返海面，隨後將馬杜羅夫婦移交至硫磺島號上的美國相關當局。
The flight leader, whom the Pentagon has not identified for security reasons, suffered serious injuries, but is recovering at a hospital in Texas along with one other soldier, the military said Tuesday. Five other service members were treated for injuries and released. Military officials described the flight leader’s actions that night as “heroic.”
美國軍方週二表示，出於安全原因，五角大廈未公布這位飛行指揮官的身份，他傷勢嚴重，但正與另一名士兵在得克薩斯州的一家醫院接受治療，目前正在康復。另有五名軍人因傷接受治療後已出院。軍方官員稱，這位飛行指揮官當晚的行為堪稱「英勇」。
The mission also resulted in the deaths of about 40 Venezuelans, in addition to 32 Cubans who were helping to guard Maduro, according to Venezuelan and Cuban officials.
據委內瑞拉和古巴官員透露，此次行動導致約40名委內瑞拉人死亡，另有32名協助守衛馬杜羅的古巴人喪生。
“This was one of the most stunning, effective and powerful displays of American military might and competence in American history,” Trump proclaimed after all the troops had returned home.
「這是美國歷史上最震撼、最有效、最強大的軍事力量與能力的展現之一，」所有士兵回國後，川普宣稱。
On Wednesday, Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth alluded to the dangers that the U.S. commandos faced. “It was a contested raid, even with the sophistication that went into it,” he told “The Charlie Kirk Show.” “It wasn’t until we saw those birds floating out that any of us could really exhale.”
週三，國防部長海格塞斯間接提及了美軍突擊隊員當時面臨的危險。「這場突襲遭遇了抵抗，即便我們做了精密準備，」他在《查理·柯克秀》節目中表示。「直到看著那些直升機順利撤離，我們才真正鬆了一口氣。」
廣告
“Not one piece of military equipment was lost,” he noted. “Not one service member was, more importantly, killed.”
「沒有損失任何軍事裝備，」他指出。「更重要的是，沒有一名軍人陣亡。」
In his news conference announcing Maduro’s capture, Trump took something of a victory lap, ticking off his recent military successes. He cited the killing of Islamic State group leader Abu Bakr al-Baghdadi by Delta Force commandos in 2019; the death of Gen. Qassem Soleimani, Iran’s top security and intelligence commander, in 2020 from a U.S. drone strike; and the targeting of Iran’s nuclear program by B-2 bombers in 2025.
在宣布抓獲馬杜羅的新聞發布會上，川普儼然一副勝利者姿態，逐一列舉他近年的軍事成就：2019年三角洲特種部隊突擊隊員擊斃伊斯蘭國組織頭目阿布·貝克爾·巴格達迪；2020年美軍無人機襲擊擊斃伊朗最高安全和情報指揮官蘇萊曼尼；2025年B-2隱形轟炸機打擊伊朗核設施。
“All perfectly executed and done,” Trump said.
「所有行動都執行得完美無缺，」川普說。
Trump quickly soured on many of the military leaders from his first term. “I’ve worked with a lot of generals,” he said after the Venezuela raid. “I worked with some I didn’t like. I worked with some I didn’t respect.”
川普在第一任期內很快就對許多軍方領導人失去了好感。「我和很多將軍共事過，」委內瑞拉突襲行動後他表示。「有些人我不喜歡，有些人我不尊重。」
By contrast, he has described Caine in glowing terms. “This guy is fantastic,” Trump said.
相比之下，他對凱恩的評價極高。「這傢伙太棒了，」川普說。
In comparison with diplomacy, which has produced slow and unsatisfying results for Trump in places like Ukraine, military action often produces quick outcomes.
在烏克蘭等地的外交努力為川普帶來的結果緩慢且缺乏成就感，而軍事行動往往能帶來快速成效。

Eric Schmitt是《紐約時報》國家安全記者，主要關注美軍事務以及海外反恐議題，他報導此類新聞已超過30年。
